## ğŸ’¡ PRO TIPS

### Content Tips
1. **Add 8-12 videos** for best showcase
2. **Mark 3-4 as featured** for homepage
3. **Use high-quality thumbnails** (800x450px minimum)
4. **Write compelling descriptions** to engage visitors
5. **Update regularly** with new content

### Design Tips
1. **Stick to 2-3 colors** for consistency
2. **Use professional photos** for about section
3. **Keep text concise** and scannable
4. **Test on mobile first** - most visitors are mobile
5. **Optimize images** before upload (compress them)

### Performance Tips
1. **Compress images** with TinyPNG or Squoosh
2. **Use YouTube** for video hosting (saves bandwidth)
3. **Limit videos** per page (use "Load More")
4. **Test loading speed** with PageSpeed Insights
5. **Enable caching** on your hosting

### SEO Tips
1. **Update meta tags** with your info
2. **Use descriptive titles** for videos
3. **Add alt text** to images
4. **Submit to Google** Search Console
5. **Share on social media** for backlinks
